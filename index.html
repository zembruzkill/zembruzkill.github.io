Currently he is Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UFRGS advised by Prof. Dr. Lisandro Zambenedetti Granville. Luciano achieved his B.Sc. Degree in Computer Science from the Integrated Regional University (URI) - Campus Frederico Westphalen, in 2017. He received his M.Sc. Degree in Computer Science from UFRGS, in 2020. His research interests include Network Functions Virtualization (NFV) , Software-Defined Networking (SDN), Internet Measurements, and Distributed Systems.
